Notebook

View, manage, create and share Notebook data.

Notebook Sharing Invitations

If you view a Notebook that you have been invited to share, you will need to accept or decline the invitation before having access to the Notebook. A sharing invitation will be displayed at the top of the page. Click the Accept button to accept the invitation to share the Notebook, or click the Decline button to refuse the invitation to share the Notebook.

Notebook Name and Description

At the top of the page is the Notebook name and description that were provided when the Notebook was created.

Notebook Table of Contents

A list of the Experiments in the Notebook is shown. Click on an Experiment name in the list to be taken to the Experiment Summary page for that Experiment. If this Notebook has been shared with you by another user, depending on the permissions you have been given by the Notebook owner you may only see Experiments you have created or you may not see anything in the Notebook Table of Contents.

Create Experiment

To create a new Experiment in this Notebook, click on the button that corresponds to the type of Experiment you would like to create. You will be taken to the Experiment Summary page for your new Experiment. The Experiment will be automatically named with the Notebook name followed by a sequential number. You are allowed to change the Experiment name. If this Notebook has been shared with you by another user, depending on the permissions you have been given by the Notebook owner you may not see the Create Experiment section and buttons. This means that you do not have permissions to create new Experiments in this Notebook.

Notebook Sharing Invitations

If you navigate to a Notebook that you have been invited to share, you will need to accept or decline the invitation before having access to the Notebook. A sharing invitation will be displayed at the top of the page. Click the Accept button to accept the invitation to share the Notebook, or click the Decline button to refuse the invitation to share the Notebook.

Share This Notebook

To share this Notebook with another user, click on Share This Notebook below the Create Experiment section and a form will open. Select which user you would like to share this Notebook with and specify the access level you would like to allow. Only the Notebook owner has permission to share a Notebook.

  • Selecting View/Read All Contents of Notebook will allow the user to view the contents of this Notebook, even if the content was created by you or another user with whom you have shared this Notebook. The user will have no ability to create new content in the Notebook with this access level.
  • Selecting Write/Create Experiments in Notebook will allow the user to create new Experiments in this Notebook. The user will have no authority to view or modify any Experiments not created by them, but will have full authority to view and modify Experiments they create.

Click the Share button to send an Invitation to the user. Your Invitation to share this Notebook will appear in the Invitations section of the user’s Dashboard with whom you shared the Notebook, as well as in the Notebook Invites section of the navigation bar on the left side of their Dashboard.

Notebook Contributors

Below the Share This Notebook section and at the bottom of the Notebook Summary page, all of the users who have shared access to this Notebook are shown. A summary of each user’s Profile is shown along with the access level they have been granted to this Notebook. Only the Notebook owner has access to this information.

Users who have Invitations to share this Notebook but who have not explicitly accepted or declined access to the Notebook are displayed with a status of “pending”. These users cannot view or create Notebook content until they have accepted your invitation to share this Notebook.

A user’s access to this Notebook can be revoked at any time by clicking the Cancel button on that user’s sharing summary. Any Notebook content that has been created by that user will remain in the Notebook, even after you have revoked their sharing privileges.

You can modify any user’s access to this Notebook by selecting a different permission set from the drop-down menu in that user’s sharing summary and clicking the Change button. If the user has already accepted your invitation to share this Notebook, the change will apply immediately. If the user has not yet accepted your invitation to share this Notebook, the new permissions will apply when they accept the sharing invitation.
